Getting Started

Step 1: Add Your First Drone

  • Click the "+ Add New Drone" button on the Fleet page
  • Type in your drone's name (like "My Freestyle Beast")
  • Tell us what you use it for: Freestyle, Cinematic, Long Range, Racing, or Trainer
  • Pick if it's "Ready to Fly" or "In Build"
  • Click "Save Drone"
  • Done! Your drone shows up on the main page

Saving Your Data (REALLY IMPORTANT!)

All your data is saved in your browser. If you clear your browser data, it's gone. So...

  • Click "Backup" once a week
  • A file downloads - save it to your computer
  • Keep it safe (OneDrive, Google Drive, USB drive, whatever)
  • If something breaks, click "Restore" and upload that file

Tips for New Users

  • Start simple - Just add drone name, role, and status. You can add more details later.
  • Use easy names - "Freestyle Beast", "Cine Queen" are better than "Quad 1" and "Quad 2"
  • Add photos - Go to imgur.com, upload a drone photo, copy the link, paste it when adding a drone
  • Backup often - Click Backup every week. Seriously.
  • Don't panic - Nothing gets deleted unless you click the red "Clear Everything" button

What Data Gets Saved?

Everything you add stays on YOUR computer. The app doesn't send anything to the internet. Your data is private.

  • Drone names, specs, photos
  • Flight history (crashes, maintenance, etc.)
  • Parts inventory
  • Betaflight configs (if you add them)

Confused? Try This

Just start clicking buttons and adding drones. You'll figure it out. The app won't break. And if you mess up, you can always restore from your backup!
