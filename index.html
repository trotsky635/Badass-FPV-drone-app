Getting Started

Step 1: Add Your First Drone

  • Click the "+ Add New Drone" button on the Fleet page
  • Type in your drone's name (like "My Freestyle Beast")
  • Tell us what you use it for: Freestyle, Cinematic, Long Range, Racing, or Trainer
  • Pick if it's "Ready to Fly" or "In Build"
  • Click "Save Drone"
  • Done! Your drone shows up on the main page
